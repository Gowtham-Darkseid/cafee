Where the

Nestled in the Eastern Ghats at 900 metres above sea level, Araku Valley is home to one of India's most extraordinary coffees. Grown under a dense canopy of native trees by indigenous tribal communities, each bean absorbs the mineral richness of volcanic soil and highland mists.

From Seed to Cup

The art of
our process

Every step is a mindful act — from the seed planted in forest shade to the cup that reaches your hands.

01

Cultivate

Seeds nurtured under native forest canopy at 900m with volcanic soil.

02

Harvest

Hand-picked at peak ripeness by skilled tribal farmers.

03

Process

Washed or natural method on elevated raised beds, slow dried.

04

Roast

Artisan roasted in small batches to order for peak freshness.

05

Deliver

Sealed, nitrogen-flushed, and shipped within 48 hours of roasting.

Our Promise

Beyond Sustainable

01

Forest Positive

Every bag sold plants three native trees in Araku Valley's degraded forest buffer zones. We have planted 2.4 million trees to date.

02

Zero Waste

Coffee husks become biochar. Water is recycled through reed beds. Our packaging is 100% home-compostable within 180 days.

03

Direct Trade

Farmers receive 40% above commodity market rates. We fund schools, health clinics, and women's cooperatives across 14 tribal villages.
